Eggfried Rice

A chinese takeaout favorite that you will soon be able to make yourself in just a handful of steps! It's extremely quick, easy and inexpensive making it the perfect meal for a busy weekday or exotic enough for date night.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice (200g)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 finely chopped carrot
  • 3 oz peas
  • 1 spring onion
  • 3 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • salt
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. If you have pre-cooked rice, skip this step. If not, first rinse the rice so it’s not going to be sticky, then we drain the rice into a large sieve
  2. Now, in a boiling pot add the rice, a tablespoon of olive oil, a teaspoon of salt ,1 and a half cups of hot water and boil everything for 10 mins at low heat with the lid on
  3. After the rice has boiled spread it in a oven tray and let it cool for 30 min – 1h
  4. Then, in a preheated pan at high heat add 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil.
  5. While the oil is heating up, beat the eggs very well and then add them into the pan. Mix and separate the eggs into small pieces. Cook this for 1 minute
  6. Then add the carrots. Cook for another minute
  7. Next add the chopped spring onion. Cook for about 10 seconds
  8. Add the cold rice and the peas. Mix and cook for another minute
  9. Then add the white pepper and mix some more
  10. Next, make space in the pan for about a third of it and add the soy sauce. Here’s the secret: let the soy sauce boil for 10 seconds then mix it with the rice. Cook for another minute while mixing, then you can serve
  11. Add more chopped spring onion over for a bit of freshness
